Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Wearablock Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Speak English application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data: Your learning progress, practice history, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Speech Data: When you use the speech recognition feature, your voice is processed by your device's built-in speech recognition service. We do not record, store, or transmit your voice data.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Voice recordings
  • Financial information

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: Our free version displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for advertising purposes. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Speech Recognition: The app uses your device's native speech recognition service (Apple Speech on iOS, Google Speech on Android), which may have its own data handling practices.

5. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security features. We recommend using device lock features (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID) to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

8. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App or clearing the app data through your device settings.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: admin@wearablock.com
